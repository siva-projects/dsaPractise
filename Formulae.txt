## Integer Arithmetic Tricks (Short List)

1. **Ceil(a/b)**: `(a + b - 1) / b`
2. **Floor(a/b)**: `a / b`
3. **Min(a,b)**: `(a + b - Math.abs(a - b)) / 2`
4. **Max(a,b)**: `(a + b + Math.abs(a - b)) / 2`
5. **Swap aâ†”b**: `a ^= b; b ^= a; a ^= b;`
6. **Power of 2**: `(n & (n-1)) == 0`
7. **Round up to m**: `(a + m - 1) / m * m`
8. **Round down to m**: `(a / m) * m`
9. **Safe modulo**: `(a % MOD * (b % MOD)) % MOD`
10. **Even**: `n & 1 == 0`
